Overview

Part 1: R Basics

The focus of the first session is to get R up and running, and to cover the basics of the object-oriented language by focusing on different data structures. In addition, we will cover how to import and export different kinds of data, download packages, and view data. From there, we'll look at the operations and cleaning text in R.This initial session will be foundational in getting situated with the R programming environment.
